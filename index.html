Toddler and Preschooler ages

Toddlers are between ages 1 to 3, and preschoolers are between ages 3-5. Most toddlers start walking at the age of three. At this age you might take them to daycare. It is important to socialize your toddler and indroduce new things and activities to them. This will help them build some social skills for the future. Around this age toddlers will develop their fine motor skills and gross motor skills which is why you must indroduce new activities to your child so they may develop those skills. The more exposed your child is to new things the more developed they will become. You must also train your child to not be overly dependent on you which could led to separation anxiety.

A toddler's health

Remember that regular medical care is important to ensure your toddler's heath, and checkups are recommended starting at around 12, 24, and 36 months old. Dental. checkups become a necessity beginning after the age of two and should continue every 6 months to maitain healthy teeth. Sleep is also essential for a child's growth. Toddlers need 10 to 15 hours of sleep each day, and preschoolers need 11 to 13 hours of sleep each day. Make sure you create a night routine before your child goes to bed to practice a good sleep schedule. Naps during the day are normal and at least one nap per day is necessary for your child. Overnight accidents, like bed-wetting, may occur and your child will grow out of this habit as he/she becomes older. Just remember not to let your child drink too much before bed to prevent this. A night lamp is also a good idea to help if your child has a fear of the dark.
